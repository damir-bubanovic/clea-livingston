Clea Charlotte Livingston

Artist

Profile

I’m a photographer, painter and digital artist. I’ve always done some form of art since I was born but it was always as a hobby. It was only in the past year that I started to take creating art seriously as more people started to show interest in what I was making.

Experience

IOM International Organisation for Migration/ Intern (paid) July - September 2019 - Pristina, Kosovo

I was the in-house photographer for whatever projects were being conducted by the organisation at the time. An example would be if someone got a grant to buy a tractor for their farm, I would accompany whoever was in charge of ‘inspection’ and take pictures as proof it was being used and document the development of what it was being used on.

Creators d.o.o./ Assistant to the director April - July 2021 - Zagreb, Croatia

I had a list of sub jobs I had to get done at any given day. In the time I worked there I was swapping from being an assistant to photographer to graphic designer to social media manager etc. Basically whatever needed to get done that day, Clea’s got it.

Education

New York Film Academy / Photography February 2017 - May 2019 - Los Angeles, California

Entered the school to study ‘Acting for Film’ but after the first semester of classes I decided it definitely wasn’t for me and Iswapped to their photography department. I stayed there until I finished schooling.

Skills

I know how to work in Adobe Lightroom, minimum knowledge of photoshop. For digital work I use Procreate and for physical painting I don’t specialise in any specific medium. I choose what material I feel like working with that day. May not come in handy but I speak 3 languages. Obviously English, and also Croatian and German.
